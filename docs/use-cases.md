# Use Cases

**Healthcare**. A hospital's diagnostic AI identifies a rare imaging pattern associated with early-stage disease. Through OCP, it shares an anonymized insight package with peer systems at other institutions. Receiving agents incorporate the pattern into their own detection workflows without any patient data changing hands.

**Financial services**. A fraud detection model at one bank identifies a novel attack vector involving micro-transaction velocity anomalies. It broadcasts an insight to bonded peers across the industry. Participating institutions harden their defenses collectively and in near real time.

**Academic research**. A literature review agent at one university discovers relevant papers and synthesizes findings into embedding vectors and insight packages. Peer agents at other universities retrieve and build on these findings, reducing duplicated effort across the research community.

**Supply chain**. Logistics AI systems at different companies form bonds to share demand forecasts and routing insights. Each agent benefits from a broader view of market conditions without exposing proprietary operational data.
